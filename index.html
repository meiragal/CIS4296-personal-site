Projects

I've worked on different projects throughout my Temple Experience :)

For MedLengua – Spanish Medical Terminology Website, a project developed in Fall 2024, I conceptualized and implemented a multi-page website aimed at improving communication between healthcare professionals and Spanish-speaking patients. This site utilized JavaScript, React, AJAX, HTML, and CSS. I developed custom features, including phonetic pronunciation guides, translation tools, slideshows, and terminology sections, all tailored specifically for pain specialists, obstetricians, and pediatricians.

In March 2024, I designed and developed the Student Registration Form, a program using WindowForms.NET in C#. This application allowed administrators to create courses and manage student registrations through a form. I integrated a Microsoft Access database with functionalities to search, add, delete, and edit courses. Additionally, I applied the Model View Controller (MVC) design pattern to handle operations such as calculating tuition costs for student registrations and displaying class lists and student rosters.

For the Vaccine Forecaster Website, created in January 2023, I developed a site that allows patients to upload their official immunization records and receive personalized vaccination recommendations based on the CDC guidelines. The website also provided resources and additional information about vaccinations, helping patients make informed decisions about their health.
